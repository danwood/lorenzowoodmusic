Santa Clause!

Santa once had pirate friends
But then they met their bitter ends.
Now he lives the pirate way
Giving & kicking booty on Christmas Day.

He's got a present you'll think is pleasant
That came from somebody who is no longer present.
Yes, it's Dottie who has passed away
But a bit of her inheritance will help you play.

So look in a car with a steering wheel.
The one we call the Grandma-mobile.
